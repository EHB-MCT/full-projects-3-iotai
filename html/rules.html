Rule 4/6

At the start of the game, everyone will be wearing a vest. When a scientist is eliminated by a predator, they will need to take off their vest and lay it down where they were eliminated.

report

Rule 5/6

If another scientist finds the vest, they can report the elimination by calling an emergency meeting.

voting

Rule 6/6

During the emergency meeting, the players need to vote for someone they think is a predator.

The player with the most votes will be eliminated.
